reservation

We take reservations in the dining room and the pub. Walk in tables are also available in the pub and garden.
If the online reservation system is showing no availability please give us a call as we will always do everything we can to accommodate you (even though the computer says no we can sometimes say yes!).
For tables of 8 or more we offer a feasting menu. Please call 020 7354 1143 or email us at

info@sokehouseislington.co.uk for more details (sample menu can be seen under ‘Menus’).

Our dining room can seat up to 60 guests and is available for private hire at certain times (minimum spend applies).
